ANITA 2018 workshop and gravitational wave astronomy school.

The 12th annual ANITA theory workshop will be held on 8th-9th February 2018 at Curtin University in Perth, Western Australia. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists.

It will be preceded by the Gravitational wave astronomy school on 5th-7th February 2018, at the University of Western Australia, in Perth, Western Australia and the Gingin Gravitational Wave Observatory.

As part of the ANITA program we asked participants to tell us what are the Big Questions in Astronomy today. These questions can be found on your badges and in this form, where you are encouraged to provide an answer.

Conference has successfully finished, thanks to all our speakers and participants. Photos can be found here and on the participants page .

ANITA Workshop 2018

The 2018 ANITA workshop will be held 8th-9th February at Curtin University in Perth, Western Australia. Talks will be held in the seminar room of ICRAR Curtin (Brodie-Hall Building).

Gravitational waves astronomy school

The 2018 gravitational wave astronomy school will run 5th-7th February at University of Western Australia in Perth, Western Australia. The school will include an excursion to the Gingin Gravitational Wave Observatory (part of the Gravitational Research Centre) and the Gravity Discovery Centre Wednesday. Monday/Tuesday will be located in the ground floor seminar room at ICRAR UWA (Ken and Julie Michael Building).
