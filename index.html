ANITA 2019 workshop and astroinformatics school.

The 13th annual Australian National Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics science workshop will be held on 7th-8th February 2019 at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Victoria, presented by Swinburne and Monash Universities. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists. Read more about ANITA here.

It will be preceded by the Astroinformatics school - "Rise of the machines" on 4th-6th February 2019, also at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Victoria.

ANITA Workshop 2019

The 2019 ANITA science workshop will be held 7th-8th February at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Victoria.

Astroinformatics school on Machine learning

The 2019 astroinformatics school will run 4th-6th February at the same location.

The theme is "Rise of the machines: An introduction to machine learning"
