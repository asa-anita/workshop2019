ANITA 2019 workshop and astroinformatics school.

The 13th annual ANITA theory workshop will be held on 7th-8th February 2019 at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Victoria. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists.

It will be preceded by the Astroinformatics school on 4th-6th February 2019, also at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Victoria.

ANITA Workshop 2019

The 2019 ANITA workshop will be held 7th-8th February at Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Victoria.

Astroinformatics school

The 2019 astroinformatics school will run 4th-6th February at the same location.
