ANITA 2020 workshop and `Cosmic explosions' summer school

The 14th annual Australian National Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics science workshop will be held on 6th-7th of February 2020 at the School of Science at UNSW Canberra. The workshop aims to provide a review of theoretical astrophysics in Australia, facilitate collaboration and build the community of theoretical astrophysicists. Read more about ANITA here.

It will be preceded by the ANITA summer school - "Cosmic explosions" on 3rd-5th of February 2020, which will also occur at the School of Science at UNSW Canberra.

ANITA Workshop 2020

The 2020 ANITA science workshop will be held on the 6th-7th of February at UNSW Canberra.

ANITA 2020 school

The 2020 ANITA summer school will run 4th-6th February at the same location.

The theme is "Cosmic explosions".
