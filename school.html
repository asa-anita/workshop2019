Astroinformatics school program - "Cosmic Explosions"

Cosmic explosions

The school will take place over three days (Mon-Wed). It will be followed by the 2020 ANITA workshop.

Topics covered will include:

  • Stellar transients:
    • Exploding massive stars
  • Gamma ray bursts and astronomy
  • Fast radio bursts
    • Including a site vist to the Molongolo telescope!

Note: We will not cover rigorous mathematical working and proofs. There simply isn't enough time in a current format. We will provide links and material that you can and should read.
