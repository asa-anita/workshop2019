Local information

Venue

The School and Workshop will take place at Swinburne University of Technology. The exact room location and directions will be announced closer to data.

Around Swinburne there are a number of shops and restaurants including Woolworths, Coles and a variety of coffee shops.

Transport

From the Melbourne airport, the best option is to take the SkyBus to Southern Cross Station in the heart of Melbourne. Fares are up to $38 (round trip) and tickets can be purchased here. From Southern Cross you can take a train towards Swinburne University on either the Lilydale or Belgrave train lines. Both of these lines stop at Glenferie station which is on the Swinburne campus. Almost all other train lines connect to Southern Cross so travelling elsewhere is also easy.

You will need to purchase a Myki card for public transport within Melbourne (including train, tram or bus). You cannot purchase single tickets with cash. Myki cards can be purchased and topped up with money at most major stations (including Southern Cross and Glenferrie). Trips cost approximately $4.80.

On the Thursday Night we will hold the workshop dinner. The location is TBD and will be paid by the individual attendees. If you have any dietary requirements please list these when you fill in your registration.

Accommodation

There are several hotels within a few minutes walking distance of Swinburne, and many more options a short train or tram ride away. The Glenferrie Hotel, Quest Hawthorn, and Richmond Hill Hotel are a few we suggest. Rooms at these hotels will be $100-$200 per night. You can find plenty more accommodation options here.
